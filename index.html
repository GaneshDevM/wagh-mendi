  1. press start game button to start the game
  2. click on any piece to move
  3. Wait for your turn
  4. there are 2 tigers vs 24 sheeps
  5. at first its turn of player who has chosen tigers side
  6. for first time, both tigers must be moved else only one can move
  7. sheeps can't kill or eat tigers they can only defence other sheeps by putting themselves behind other sheeps in line of attack of tiger
  8. tiger can kill a lone sheep just in front of or side of it by moving itself to a blank space ahead of that sheep
  9. at starting only 8 of the sheeps are placed
  10. at turn of sheeps you have to put new sheeps till its all over in the field
  11. when there is no move left for tiger then ship wins or if all sheeps are dead then tiger wins
